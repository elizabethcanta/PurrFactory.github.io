📖 How to Play

Welcome to Purr Factory! Collect Purrs by petting cats, buy upgrades, and unlock achievements on your way to becoming the ultimate Cat Whisperer.

🖱️ Basic Controls

Click the cat in the centre to earn Purrs. Spend Purrs in the Upgrade Shop to increase your click value or unlock auto-petting devices. Watch your combo multiplier — click fast to chain purrs!

⚡ Combo System

Click rapidly to build a Combo. Each combo level multiplies your purrs per click (up to ×5). The combo resets if you stop clicking for 2 seconds.

🛒 Upgrade List

  • Ear Scratch — +1 Purr/click (base price: 10)
  • Chin Rub — +3 Purrs/click (base price: 50)
  • Belly Rub — +8 Purrs/click (base price: 180)
  • Full-Body Massage — +25 Purrs/click (base price: 600)
  • Robo-Petter Mk I — Auto-pets every 2 s (base price: 120)
  • Robo-Petter Mk II — Upgrades to every 0.75 s (base price: 400)
